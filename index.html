Let's Play Crystal Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific number to your Total Score.

You win the game by matching your Total Score to the Random Number exactly. If your Total Score goes over the Random Number, you lose.

The value of each crystal's number will be hidden from you.

Each time the game starts, the crystals will be given new values and the Random Number will change.
